Finding the Signal

It appears to be very difficult to come to a decisive conclusion of ‘right’ or ‘wrong’ when it comes to selecting books for oneself, or another person.

We are all on our own, highly individualised, subjective journeys through life and a ‘terrible’ book written off by the masses on the internet might just be the very source you were looking for all along.

Here at The Offline Space, we cannot promise to present you The Book(s) you were desperately searching for, but we strive to provide a curated selection of time tested, publicly vetted books that have quality material and a high probability of striking a chord.
