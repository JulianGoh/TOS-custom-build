In a world with too much information, there comes about a lack of clarity on how best to use this knowledge in order to deal with the present and to move into the future.

Then the question becomes: How should I learn?

This is The Offline Space.

How?

Fast food. Fast information. Fast responses.

We all lost touch with patience a long time ago, and nowadays it is easier than ever to absorb and succumb to whatever shows up first on our screens.

The Offline Space seeks out all that which is slow.

Old, timeless knowledge that has been filtered through the tests of reality and human experience.

How might we do this?

Through books.

What?

An automated bookstore and learning hub accessible by day or by night.



The Vision:

Today, we live in a frantic, modern world where information is fast, cheap and abundant but knowledge is scarce.

We believe in access to slow, timeless knowledge that has been filtered through the tests of time and human experience.



The Mission:

We are builders of communities for learning.

Through access to time tested books, The Offline Space seeks to carve out and cultivate a hub for the individual to learn well, and a community to do so alongside with.
